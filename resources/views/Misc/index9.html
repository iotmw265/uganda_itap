About IoT.mw

A little about us.

Introducing IoT.mw....

A sub-division of iMoSyS Limited, IoT.mw is an Internet-of-Things analytics platform that allows users to aggregate, visualize and analyze live data streams in the cloud. Field devices connect to IoT.mw to analyze and process fast moving streams of data that can be used to get insights, build reports or trigger alerts and actions.


We exist to assist industries and individuals in reducing process running costs, improve productivity, reveal inefficiencies in supply chains and reinforce decision making by introduction of timely information through production reports, sales reports, remote circumstances, alarms and in many other applications.
